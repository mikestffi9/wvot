Northbrook, IL: A Haven for Outdoor Living

Northbrook, IL, is a thriving suburban community located just north of Chicago, known for its picturesque natural beauty and ideal location for outdoor living. The village is surrounded by a plethora of natural attractions, including the world-renowned Chicago Botanic Garden in Glencoe and the River Trail Nature Center in Northbrook. These nearby green spaces serve as a constant source of inspiration for residents, encouraging them to embrace the outdoors and create inviting living spaces on their own properties.

The Chicago Botanic Garden, spanning an impressive 385 acres, features 27 distinct gardens and four natural areas, showcasing a diverse collection of plants from around the world. The garden hosts numerous events, classes, and workshops throughout the year, attracting visitors from far and wide. Similarly, the River Trail Nature Center offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, with its winding trails, educational exhibits, and diverse wildlife.

The proximity to these natural wonders, combined with the community's commitment to preserving green spaces, makes Northbrook an ideal location for those seeking to create their own outdoor oasis. The village's well-maintained parks, trails, and public spaces provide a strong sense of connection to nature, which is reflected in the pride homeowners take in their own outdoor living spaces.


Benefits of Customized Landscape Solutions

Customized landscape solutions offer a wide array of benefits to homeowners and businesses. By investing in professional landscape design, Northbrook property owners can enhance the value and aesthetic appeal of their outdoor spaces while creating functional areas for relaxation, entertainment, and connection with nature.

One of the primary advantages of professional landscape design is the ability to create visually appealing and cohesive outdoor environments. A well-designed landscape takes into account factors such as the property's architecture, natural surroundings, and the client's personal style and preferences. By carefully selecting plants, materials, and design elements, landscape designers can create a harmonious and inviting atmosphere that complements the property and enhances its overall beauty.

In addition to aesthetic benefits, customized landscape solutions can significantly improve the functionality of outdoor spaces. Professional designers incorporate practical elements such as patios, walkways, and garden beds, which maximize the usability of the area and create designated zones for various activities. For example, a well-designed patio with comfortable seating and a fire pit can serve as a perfect spot for entertaining guests or enjoying a relaxing evening outdoors.


Furthermore, experienced landscape designers prioritize the use of native plants and sustainable features when creating custom landscapes. Native plants are adapted to the local climate and soil conditions, requiring less water, fertilizer, and maintenance compared to non-native species. By incorporating these plants into the landscape design, property owners can enjoy a beautiful, low-maintenance outdoor space that supports local biodiversity and minimizes environmental impact.

Sustainable landscaping practices extend beyond plant selection and include techniques such as rainwater harvesting, permeable pavers, and organic pest control methods. These eco-friendly approaches not only contribute to a healthier environment but also result in long-term cost savings for the property owner by reducing water consumption, minimizing the need for chemical treatments, and preventing costly repairs associated with poor drainage or soil erosion.

Sustainable and Eco-Friendly Landscaping Practices

In addition to its commitment to craftsmanship and customer satisfaction, Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design is a strong proponent of sustainable and eco-friendly landscaping practices. The company recognizes the vital role that landscape design plays in promoting environmental stewardship and strives to incorporate sustainable techniques and materials into every project.


One of the most important aspects of sustainable landscaping is the selection of native plants. Native species are naturally adapted to the local climate and soil conditions, requiring less water, fertilizer, and pesticides compared to non-native plants. By incorporating a diverse array of native plants into their designs, Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design creates landscapes that are not only beautiful but also support local biodiversity and contribute to a healthier ecosystem.

In addition to native plant selection, the company can help clients with a variety of other eco-friendly landscaping techniques and ideas, such as:


  • Permeable Pavers: Unlike traditional concrete or asphalt surfaces, permeable pavers allow water to penetrate the ground, reducing runoff and preventing soil erosion. Using permeable pavers in the construction of patios, walkways, and driveways to promote better drainage and minimize the environmental impact of hardscaping.
  • Rainwater Harvesting: By installing rainwater collection systems, such as rain barrels or cisterns, property owners can capture and store rainwater for later use in irrigation. This practice conserves water resources, reduces reliance on municipal water supplies, and can lead to significant cost savings over time.
  • Organic Fertilizers and Pest Control: The use of organic fertilizers and natural pest control methods over synthetic chemicals. These eco-friendly alternatives promote healthier soil and plants while minimizing the risk of harmful chemical runoff and protecting local wildlife.
  • Recycled and Sustainable Materials: In the construction of hardscaping features and outdoor structures, the company opts for recycled and sustainable materials whenever possible. For example, using reclaimed wood for a deck or locally sourced stone for a retaining wall can reduce the environmental impact of the project and support the local economy.


By implementing these sustainable landscaping practices, homeowners create beautiful and functional outdoor spaces and also contribute to the overall health and well-being of the environment.


Clients who choose to work with the company can feel confident that their landscape design project will have a positive impact on the local ecosystem and serve as an example of responsible environmental stewardship.
