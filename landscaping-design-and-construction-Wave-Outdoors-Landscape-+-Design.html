About Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design: Transforming Chicago's Outdoor Spaces

Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design creates stunning, large-scale outdoor environments for clients in the Chicago area. This article explores the company's unique approach to landscape design, hardscape installation, and project management, showing how they turn expansive properties into beautiful outdoor living spaces.


Innovative Landscape Design for Expansive Spaces

Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design brings creativity and precision to large-scale landscape projects. They ensure that even the biggest properties feel cohesive and inviting. By using advanced 3D design technology, Wave Outdoors helps clients see their dream landscapes before any work begins.

The company's designers are experts at creating different environmental zones within a single property. This makes large outdoor spaces more functional and appealing. It also helps with energy efficiency by cleverly placing elements that control temperature and wind patterns.

Wave Outdoors loves using native plants in their designs. This not only looks great but also helps local wildlife. It fits perfectly with Chicago's focus on green spaces and taking care of the environment.


  • Native plants often used in Chicago landscape designs:
  • Big Bluestem Grass
  • Purple Coneflower
  • Illinois Bundleflower
  • Wild Bergamot

The company is great at working with all kinds of land, from flat city lots to hilly suburban areas. They make sure each project tells a unique story that matches the client's personality and the natural features of the property.


Hardscape Installation: Building Beautiful, Long-lasting Outdoor Structures

Hardscaping is a big part of Wave Outdoors' large-scale projects. They're experts at making different hardscape elements work together, creating outdoor spaces that look great and last a long time. They carefully choose materials that can handle Chicago's tough winters and hot summers.

Hardscape Material Benefits Best Use in Chicago Climate Natural Stone Durability, Looks Great Patios, Walkways Permeable Pavers Handles Rain Well, Not Slippery Driveways, Large Patios Concrete (Stamped, Colored) Versatile, Cost-Effective Pool Decks, Outdoor Kitchens Cor-Ten Steel Modern Look, Weather Resistant Retaining Walls, Decorative Elements Wave Outdoors is skilled at creating multi-level features that make large areas more interesting and useful. This works especially well for sloped properties, which are common in Chicago's varied landscape.

The company thinks carefully about heat when planning hardscapes. They know how to keep large paved areas comfortable during Chicago's warm summers. They choose materials wisely and add shading to make outdoor spaces enjoyable even on hot days.

Wave Outdoors is also great at managing water in their hardscape designs. This is crucial in Chicago, where heavy rains and melting snow can cause problems. They use permeable surfaces and smart drainage solutions to keep large hardscape areas working well and looking good all year.


Spotlight on Specific Hardscape Projects

Retaining Walls

Wave Outdoors excels in designing and building retaining walls that are both functional and attractive. In Chicago's varied terrain, retaining walls play a crucial role in managing slopes and creating usable outdoor space. The company uses a range of materials, from natural stone to modern concrete blocks, to create walls that complement the overall landscape design. They pay special attention to drainage behind the walls, ensuring long-term stability in Chicago's freeze-thaw climate.


Swimming Pools

The company's pool designs go beyond simple rectangles. Wave Outdoors creates custom pool shapes that fit naturally into the landscape, often incorporating features like beach entries, infinity edges, or adjoining spas. They understand the importance of pool surrounds, designing decks and patios that provide both safety and style. In the Chicago climate, they also focus on energy-efficient pool equipment and covers to extend the swimming season.


Decks and Patios

Wave Outdoors designs decks and patios that serve as true outdoor living spaces. They often use a mix of materials – like wood and stone – to create visual interest. For decks, they favor durable, low-maintenance materials that can withstand Chicago's weather extremes. Patios are designed with subtle slopes for proper drainage, and often include built-in features like fire pits or outdoor kitchens.


Outdoor Rooms

Taking the concept of outdoor living to the next level, Wave Outdoors creates fully-equipped outdoor rooms. These spaces might include covered areas for shade, outdoor fireplaces for warmth on cool Chicago evenings, and built-in seating for comfort. The company pays close attention to lighting design in these spaces, ensuring they're usable well into the evening hours.


Outdoor Kitchens

Recognizing the growing trend of outdoor cooking, Wave Outdoors designs comprehensive outdoor kitchen spaces. These go beyond a simple grill, often including built-in refrigerators, sinks, and ample counter space. They choose materials that can stand up to Chicago's weather while still looking great, like stainless steel appliances and weather-resistant cabinetry.


Water Features

From simple fountains to elaborate waterfalls, Wave Outdoors incorporates water features that add sound and movement to the landscape. In Chicago's urban areas, these features can help mask city noise. The company designs water features with winter in mind, often including options for easy winterization to prevent damage from freezing temperatures.


Project Management: Handling Complex Landscapes

Wave Outdoors is great at managing large-scale landscape projects. They're good at coordinating different teams and materials, which is important for making complex projects run smoothly. This is especially helpful in Chicago, where the short construction season means timing is crucial.

The company breaks big projects into smaller, manageable parts. This helps them keep track of progress and adjust for Chicago's unpredictable weather. It also helps with budgeting, letting clients spread costs over time without changing the overall plan.

Wave Outdoors is skilled at working with clients, suppliers, and city officials. This helps them navigate Chicago's complicated permit processes and follow local rules. They're good at spotting potential problems early and solving them before they affect project timelines or budgets.

Project Management Aspect Wave Outdoors' Approach Benefit to Chicago Clients Resource Coordination Organized team and material management Projects run efficiently Project Breakdown Dividing projects into phases Can adjust for weather changes Communication Proactive talks with all involved parties Smooth handling of local rules Problem Solving Planning for potential issues Fewer delays and budget surprises Wave Outdoors has strict quality control measures. They maintain high standards across all parts of their large projects, ensuring consistent, reliable results. This is especially important in Chicago, where big temperature changes can test how well landscape installations hold up.


Sustainable Practices in Chicago Landscaping

Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design focuses on eco-friendly practices in their projects. This fits well with Chicago's growing interest in environmental responsibility. By using native plants and sustainable materials, Wave Outdoors creates landscapes that look beautiful and help the local ecosystem.


  • Key sustainable practices used by Wave Outdoors:
  • Planting native species
  • Using water-efficient irrigation systems
  • Including permeable surfaces to manage rainwater
  • Using recycled materials in hardscaping

The company's approach to promoting biodiversity goes beyond just looks. By creating diverse plant communities, Wave Outdoors helps support local wildlife and improves the overall health of the Chicago area's ecosystem. This focus on biodiversity also results in landscapes that better resist pests and diseases, reducing the need for chemicals.

Wave Outdoors' commitment to sustainability extends to their hardscape installations too. They prioritize using locally sourced materials, which reduces transportation-related pollution. Their expertise in designing permeable surfaces also helps with urban runoff issues, which is important in Chicago where managing stormwater is a priority.


Technological Integration in Landscape Design

Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design uses cutting-edge technology to improve their design and implementation processes. Their 3D landscape design tools let clients see their projects in amazing detail before any work starts. This is especially valuable for big projects, where understanding space and views can be challenging.

The company also uses advanced irrigation technology to create sustainable landscapes. Smart irrigation systems adjust watering schedules based on weather conditions and plant needs, helping to save water while keeping plants healthy. This is particularly beneficial in the Chicago area, where water conservation is becoming increasingly important.

Wave Outdoors also uses technology in their project management. Advanced scheduling and resource allocation tools help them work more efficiently, which is especially valuable during Chicago's short construction season.


Adapting to Chicago's Unique Climate Challenges

Chicago's climate makes landscape design and maintenance tricky. Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design is great at creating outdoor spaces that thrive in this demanding environment. They make sure their landscapes look good and work well through all of Chicago's distinct seasons.

Season Climate Challenge Wave Outdoors' Solution Winter Harsh cold, lots of snow Salt-resistant plants, heated hardscapes Spring Quick temperature changes, heavy rain Good drainage systems, frost-resistant plants Summer Heat waves, possible drought Plants that need less water, smart shade planning Fall Early frost, lots of leaves Plants that handle cold well, easy leaf management Wave Outdoors is particularly good at creating comfortable microclimates. They carefully consider things like wind patterns, sun exposure, and how close the property is to Lake Michigan. This helps them create outdoor spaces that stay comfortable for longer periods throughout the year. It also helps with energy efficiency by moderating the climate around buildings.

The company chooses plants that can handle Chicago's extreme temperatures, from freezing winters to hot, humid summers. This careful selection ensures that landscapes stay beautiful and healthy year-round, without needing lots of replacements or extensive care.


Innovation in Outdoor Living Trends

Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design stays at the forefront of outdoor living trends, constantly innovating to meet the evolving needs of Chicago residents. They recognize that outdoor spaces are increasingly seen as extensions of the home, and design them accordingly.


  • Emerging trends incorporated by Wave Outdoors:
  • Multi-functional spaces that adapt to different uses
  • Integration of smart home technology in outdoor areas
  • Creation of outdoor work-from-home spaces
  • Design of year-round outdoor entertainment areas

The company excels in creating outdoor spaces that blend seamlessly with indoor living areas. They often incorporate large sliding or folding doors that allow for an easy transition between inside and outside. This approach is particularly appealing in Chicago, where residents want to maximize their enjoyment of the outdoors during the pleasant months.

Wave Outdoors also focuses on extending the usability of outdoor spaces beyond the traditional summer season. They incorporate features like outdoor heaters, wind screens, and covered areas to make spaces comfortable even in cooler weather. This approach allows Chicago residents to enjoy their outdoor areas for more months of the year.

The company's innovative approach extends to their use of materials as well. They often experiment with new, sustainable materials that offer improved durability and reduced maintenance needs. This focus on innovation ensures that Wave Outdoors' projects remain at the cutting edge of landscape design.


Conclusion: Transforming Chicago's Outdoor Spaces

Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design brings a unique mix of creativity, technical know-how, and forward-thinking to large-scale landscape projects in the Chicago area. They're great at designing, building hardscapes, and managing projects, which allows them to create outdoor spaces that are beautiful, functional, sustainable, and long-lasting.

By embracing new technology, focusing on sustainability, and really understanding Chicago's unique climate challenges, Wave Outdoors is a leader in the landscape design industry. Their commitment to quality, attention to detail, and focus on making clients happy ensure that each project they do makes Chicago's urban and suburban landscapes better.

For property owners in the Chicago area who want to transform their outdoor spaces, Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design offers a great combination of expertise, innovation, and dedication to excellence. As they continue to shape the region's outdoor environments, they play a key role in making Chicago's diverse landscapes more beautiful, functional, and environmentally friendly.
