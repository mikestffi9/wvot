The Benefits of a Custom Deck

A custom deck offers numerous advantages over a standard, one-size-fits-all deck. Personalized design: Your deck is designed to complement your home's architecture and reflect your unique style and taste. 3. Enhanced entertainment: A well-designed deck creates the perfect setting for hosting gatherings, grilling, or simply relaxing with family and friends. 4. Our Comprehensive Deck Installation Process

At Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design, we follow a meticulous process to ensure that your custom deck is built to the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship. Our deck installation process includes the following key steps: 1. Consultation: We begin with a free, on-site consultation to discuss your goals, preferences, and budget. 2. Design: Our designers create a detailed 3D model of your custom deck, incorporating your feedback and making revisions until you're completely satisfied. 3. Permitting: We handle all necessary permits and ensure compliance with local building codes and regulations. 4. Site preparation: Our team prepares the site, including excavation and grading, to create a stable foundation for your deck. 5. Installation: Our skilled deck builders construct your deck using proven techniques and quality materials, paying meticulous attention to detail. 6. Final walkthrough: We conduct a final inspection with you to ensure your complete satisfaction with your new deck. Throughout the entire process, our team maintains clear communication, providing updates and addressing any concerns or questions you may have.

Choosing the Right Materials for Your Custom Deck

Selecting the appropriate materials for your custom deck is crucial for its longevity, appearance, and maintenance requirements. At Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design, we offer a wide range of high-quality decking materials to suit your preferences and budget, including: - Premium wood - Natural beauty and warmth, with options like cedar, redwood, and ipe - Timeless aesthetic - Can be stained or painted to match your style - Composite decking - Low-maintenance, durable, and available in a variety of colors and textures - Resistant to fading, staining, and moisture damage - Requires minimal upkeep - PVC decking - Superior resistance to moisture, insects, and fading, with a wide range of design options - Extremely durable and long-lasting - Easy to clean and maintain Our experts will guide you through the pros and cons of each material, helping you make an informed decision that best suits your needs and preferences.
