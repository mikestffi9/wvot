Yard Grading: Ensuring Proper Drainage and Protection

Proper yard grading is essential for ensuring adequate drainage and protecting your home's foundation. Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design offers expert yard grading services that address drainage issues and prevent erosion.


Addressing Drainage Issues

Poor drainage can lead to a host of problems, including standing water, soil erosion, and damage to your home's foundation. Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design uses advanced grading techniques to direct water away from your home and prevent pooling in low-lying areas.


Erosion Prevention

Erosion can cause significant damage to your landscape, washing away topsoil and exposing tree roots. Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design uses erosion control measures such as retaining walls, ground covers, and proper grading to prevent erosion and maintain the stability of your landscape.


Foundation Protection

Improper grading can allow water to seep into your home's foundation, causing cracks, leaks, and other structural damage. Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design ensures that your yard is graded to direct water away from your foundation, protecting your home from costly water damage.

Irrigation & Stormwater Management: Conservation and Compliance

Proper irrigation and stormwater management are essential for maintaining a healthy and sustainable landscape. Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design offers innovative solutions for irrigation and stormwater management that conserve water and comply with local regulations.


Water Conservation Techniques

Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design uses a variety of water conservation techniques to reduce water usage and costs. These techniques include:

- Drip irrigation

- Rainwater harvesting

- Xeriscaping

- Hydrozoning


By using these techniques, Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design helps clients maintain a beautiful landscape while conserving water and reducing their environmental impact.


Smart Irrigation Systems

Wave Outdoors Landscape + Design installs smart irrigation systems that use advanced technology to optimize watering schedules and reduce water waste. These systems include features such as:

- Weather-based controllers

- Soil moisture sensors

- Flow sensors

- Remote monitoring and control


Smart irrigation systems ensure that your landscape receives the right amount of water at the right time, reducing water waste and promoting healthy plant growth.


Compliance with Local Regulations
